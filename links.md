# Links & Bookmarks

Welcome to my public bookmarks. Here you'll find a curated list of tools, articles, and resources that I find interesting or useful in my day-to-day work.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

* [Anthropic Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research) - Great papers on AI alignment and safety.
* [Epoch AI](https://epochai.org/) - Research and data on the trajectory of AI development.
* [LessWrong](https://www.lesswrong.com/) - A community blog devoted to refining the art of human rationality.

## Software Engineering

* [Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/) - Essential daily reading for tech news.
* [The Pragmatic Engineer](https://blog.pragmaticengineer.com/) - Excellent insights into Big Tech and high-growth startups.
* [Lobsters](https://lobste.rs/) - A computing-focused community.

## Design & UI/UX

* [Awwwards](https://www.awwwards.com/) - Great inspiration for modern web design.
* [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) - A utility-first CSS framework (though I prefer custom CSS for this blog!).
* [Refactoring UI](https://www.refactoringui.com/) - Practical design tips for developers.

## Interesting Reads

* [Effective Altruism](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/) - Doing good better.
* [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/) - Research and data to make progress against the world’s largest problems.
* [Astral Codex Ten](https://astralcodexten.substack.com/) - Scott Alexander's blog on reasoning, science, and psychiatry.
